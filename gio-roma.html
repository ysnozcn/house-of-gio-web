ABOUT GIO ROMA

Gio Roma is a chic luxury loft apartment in the heart of Rome, located on the quiet and verdant Oppian Hill—just a few minutes’ walk from the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and Monti’s celebrated restaurants and boutiques.

Set within an elegant late-1700s palazzo, the residence features its own private ground-floor entrance, ensuring comfort, independence, and total privacy.

With its blend of Roman heritage, sophisticated design, and effortless comfort, Gio Roma offers an exclusive urban retreat for guests seeking a truly exceptional stay in the historic center of Rome.
