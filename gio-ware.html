GIO WARE

CRAFTED WITH HERITAGE

Rooted in Tuscany. Shared with love.

Welcome to Gio Ware, the lifestyle extension of our House of Gio world — where Italian craftsmanship, Tuscan agricultural tradition, and quiet luxury meet.

Gio Ware is both our online boutique and a tiny, elegant physical boutique located inside the Clubhouse at Gio Ranch. It is a place where the essence of our land takes shape: delicious things we grow, beautiful things we make, and thoughtful pieces we curate.

From our lovingly harvested olive oil and honey to artisanal treasures crafted in Italy, every item tells the story of our estate and the people who bring it to life.

Gio Ware also houses Giovanni, our intimate agriristo — an agricultural restaurant serving heartfelt farm-to-table meals, prepared with produce from our land, neighbouring farms, and the very best ingredients sourced across Tuscany.

BOUTIQUE

Authentic flavours. Artisanal treasures. True Tuscan heritage.


Our boutique celebrates the beauty of things made slowly, thoughtfully, and with purpose. Explore a curated selection of estate-grown products and authentic artisanal pieces made in Italy — all reflecting our passion for craftsmanship, agriculture, and refined simplicity.

ESTATE OLIVE OIL –
IGP TOSCANA

Hand-picked, cold-pressed, and 100% organic, our olive oil is produced in limited quantities and carries the prestigious IGP Toscana certification. Vibrant, green, and elegantly balanced – a pure expression of our land.

“I LOVE YOU HONEY” –
ESTATE HONEY

Harvested from beehives nestled between our olive groves, this honey is naturally floral, aromatic, and made in small batches. A sweet love letter from the estate.

THE CLUBHOUSE

The Clubhouse Agriristo — Tuscan cooking from the heart.

Giovanni is our warm, welcoming agriristo — a farm restaurant reserved exclusively for our staying and riding guests. Here, meals are cooked with intention, generosity, and respect for the land. Expect soulful dishes, fresh produce, rustic elegance, and the kind of hospitality that feels like home.

Our menus change with the seasons and reflect what the land gives us: vegetables from our garden, meats and cheeses from trusted neighbouring farms, and traditional Tuscan ingredients that honour local heritage.

PET POLICY

The ranch is shared with free-roaming dogs, cats, horses, and local wildlife. To ensure the safety of all animals and guests:

  • Visiting dogs are not permitted to join trail rides.
  • Pets may access the ranch only if kept on a leash and under constant supervision at all times.

What to Wear

Jeans or long trousers, socks, and comfortable sport or hiking shoes.

We recommend sunscreen and insect repellent, especially in summer.
