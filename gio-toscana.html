GIO TOSCANA

BACK-TO-NATURE

Hidden in one of the most secluded valleys of the Maremma, just 15 km from the renowned beaches of Castiglione della Pescaia, Punta Ala and Cala Violina, Gio Toscana offers an intimate and poetic agriturismo experience.

At the heart of the estate stands the Manor House, a charming country residence that embraces three independent apartments – Manor I, Manor II and Manor III. Each apartment is fully furnished, curated with understated elegance and comfort, and offers its own private garden or terrace for quiet mornings, lingering breakfasts and unforgettable Tuscan sunsets.

In front of the Manor house lies a panaromic swimming pool overlooking rolling hills, olive groves and the endless Tuscan sky — a peaceful retreat for guests who seek beauty, quiet, and the authentic warmth of country life.

SWIMMING POOL GUIDELINES

The private swimming pool is reserved exclusively for the guests of the property and the owners.
Outside visitors are not permitted unless expressly authorized by Management.

Pool hours are from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

There is no lifeguard on duty. The pool is unsupervised. Guests swim at their own risk and are asked to exercise maximum caution.

A shower is required before entering the pool. Entering the water with sunscreen, tanning products, or hair oils without showering is not permitted.

Swimming during adverse weather conditions is prohibited.

Smoking is not allowed in the pool area.

Guests who are not confident swimmers must use appropriate flotation devices.

Towels provided in the rooms may not be used at the pool. Littering of any kind in the pool or garden area is strictly prohibited.

Glass, metal, hard plastic containers, and any objects not directly related to swimming are not allowed in the pool area.

Running, pushing others into the water, and diving are strictly prohibited. The pool depth does not allow diving and this is extremely dangerous.

Games or activities that may disturb or endanger other guests are not permitted.

Loud behavior is not allowed. Guests are requested to maintain a calm and respectful atmosphere at all times.

Children and teenagers under 18 years of age must be accompanied and supervised by a responsible adult at all times.

Infants and toddlers must wear appropriate swim diapers.

When alone in the pool area, guests are advised not to enter the water.

Animals are not permitted in the pool or surrounding areas.
Inflatable toys and air mattresses are not allowed.

The swimming pool measures 5 m x 10 m (16.4 ft x 32.8 ft) with a uniform depth of 1.40 m (4.6 ft).

By accessing the pool, guests fully accept these rules, which are available in each room/apartment, displayed in the pool area, and published on the website.

Use of the pool is entirely at the guest’s own risk. The property, management, and other guests are released from any civil or criminal liability for accidents, injuries, or damages, particularly in cases of non-compliance with these rules.

The Management assumes no responsibility for personal belongings or valuables left in the pool area.

To ensure safety, cleanliness, and a pleasant experience for all, guests are kindly asked to act with common sense, courtesy, and respect.

PET POLICY

Please note that the estate is home to dogs, cats, horses, and other animals. All resident animals are friendly with people and other animals, are regularly vaccinated, and move freely within the property. Guests acknowledge that the management cannot be held responsible for the safety or wellbeing of visiting pets.

For safety and hygiene reasons:

  • Pets must always be kept on a leash in the pool area, around the accommodations and other buildings, and when visiting the horse paddocks and stables.
  • Pets must not urinate or defecate on the lawn areas surrounding the apartments and the pool.
  • Pets must not be left unattended in the apartments or anywhere on the estate when owners are absent.
  • Feeding of resident animals is strictly prohibited.
