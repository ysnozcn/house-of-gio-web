GIO TOSCANA

BACK-TO-NATURE

Hidden in one of the most secluded valleys of the Maremma, just 15 km from the renowned beaches of Castiglione della Pescaia, Punta Ala and Cala Violina, Gio Toscana offers an intimate and poetic agriturismo experience.

At the heart of the estate stands the Manor House, a charming country residence that embraces three independent apartments – Manor I, Manor II and Manor III. Each apartment is fully furnished, curated with understated elegance and comfort, and offers its own private garden or terrace for quiet mornings, lingering breakfasts and unforgettable Tuscan sunsets.

In front of the Manor house lies a panaromic swimming pool overlooking rolling hills, olive groves and the endless Tuscan sky — a peaceful retreat for guests who seek beauty, quiet, and the authentic warmth of country life.
