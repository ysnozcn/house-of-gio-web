THE STORY BEHIND HOUSE OF GIO

The House of Gio was born from a feeling rather than a plan: a love for beautiful things, meaningful encounters, and the Italian art of living.

The name “Gio” honours Giovanni, an ancestor whose laughter filled every room and whose Tuscan heart celebrated nature, food, friendship and culture. From him we learned the joy of welcoming others — and so our “house” became more than a place; it became a way of being.
