HOUSE OF GIO ESTATE SOUTHERN TUSCANY

Just 15 km from Castiglione della Pescaia, the estate is owned and operated by Società Agricola Giò Ranch Società Semplice (registered locally).

Commercial activities fall within the estate’s agricultural and agriturismo framework — agriturismo, servizi agrituristici, agriristoro, degustazioni, and the sale of estate products (including olive oil).
