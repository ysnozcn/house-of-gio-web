THE SPIRIT OF THE WEST IN THE

HEART OF TUSCANY

Luxury, bespoke riding experiences in authentic western saddle, immersed in Tuscany’s unspoiled nature—where freedom, elegance, and authenticity ride as one.

Gio Ranch is a western-style horseback riding farm set within the magnificent House of Gio Estate, just 15 km from Castiglione della Pescaia in southern Tuscany. Surrounded by one of Maremma’s most unspoiled valleys, Gio Ranch offers an authentic western experience with expert ENGEA-certified guides, custom Italian western saddles, and a herd of exceptionally well-trained American Quarter Horses.

Our riding escapes are designed for all levels — from confident beginners to experienced riders — with programs that celebrate nature, culture, gastronomy, and the quiet rhythm of country life.

Custom-made Le Selle Italiane western saddles (with sheepskin seat savers for extra comfort)
High-quality tack and equipment
Helmets (mandatory)

PET POLICY

The ranch is shared with free-roaming dogs, cats, horses, and local wildlife. To ensure the safety of all animals and guests:

  • Visiting dogs are not permitted to join trail rides.
  • Pets may access the ranch only if kept on a leash and under constant supervision at all times.

What to Wear

Jeans or long trousers, socks, and comfortable sport or hiking shoes.

We recommend sunscreen and insect repellent, especially in summer.
